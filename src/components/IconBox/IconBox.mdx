# Icon Box
<p>
  A boxed icon is an icon that is contained within a shape, typically a square or a rectangle. This shape, or "box," serves as a background or container for the icon, providing visual emphasis and context. Boxed icons are commonly used in UI/UX design to create visually distinct and cohesive elements within interfaces. They help to organize and group related icons or content, while also adding a sense of structure and consistency to the design.
</p>
