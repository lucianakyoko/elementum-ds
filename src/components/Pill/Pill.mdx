# Pill
<p>
  Pills in UI/UX design are small, rounded rectangular elements often used to represent items such as tags, categories, or navigation options. They typically contain text or icons and are visually distinct from surrounding elements due to their shape and often contrasting background color.
</p>

## Pill props
- text,
- variant,
- color,
- corner,
- size
